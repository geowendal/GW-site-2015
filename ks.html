KUALI STUDENT COURSE REGISTRATION

The Kuali Foundation creates community-source product suites designed to serve the needs of higher-education institutions. The development teams building these products are typically comprised of individuals employed by the different universities that are members of the Foundation. In my role at the University of Maryland, I was tasked with leading the design of the Kuali Student Course Registration System - the system that students would use to reigster for courses. Course Registration interacts with existing administrative Kuali Student systems (Curriculum Management and Course Offerings) to deliver a top-to-bottom solution for setting up and administering classes at the university level.
