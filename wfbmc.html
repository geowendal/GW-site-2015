WAKE FOREST BAPTIST MEDICAL CENTER

What I did: Content Inventory, Site Map, Information Architecture

Wake Forest Baptist Medical Center's original intranet had grown unwieldy, and they wanted a complete overhaul of the design. This project started with some in-depth interviews with stakeholders to thoroughly understand the business goals, and then a deep dive into the existing site to inventory and categorize the content.

The original intranet had grown to over 800 pages, and the navigation structure that was in place made it difficult for users to find the information they were looking for. I went through all 800 pages of the original site and identified templates to accomodate the content, then mocked up wireframe versions of the templates.

After approval, the wireframes were handed off for visual design. The content from the original intranet was migrated over, and the new intranet site was successfully launched.
